Dispute Resolution

Before initiating formal proceedings, the parties will attempt in good faith to resolve disputes through discussions between executives.

Arbitration

Except for claims for injunctive relief or IP protection, any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement will be finally resolved by binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association (AAA) under its Commercial Arbitration Rules (or, if Customer is an individual consumer, the Consumer Rules). One arbitrator with at least ten (10) years' commercial technology experience will preside. Arbitration will be conducted virtually (video, telephonic, or written submissions), with the legal seat deemed Wilmington, Delaware. Proceedings will be in English. Judgment on the award may be entered in any court of competent jurisdiction.

Class Action Waiver

Disputes must be brought in the parties' individual capacities and not as plaintiffs or class members in any purported class or representative proceeding. If the class waiver is held unenforceable, this arbitration provision is void.
